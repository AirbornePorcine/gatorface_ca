## About the Piece

A custom jewelry display stand crafted from a single slab of Arbutus. The raised feet at each end allow the board to stand upright - the client adds pins to the top edge for hanging necklaces.

| Wood              | Finish                          | Other Details                                                    |
|:------------------:|:--------------------------------:|:---------------------------------------------------------:|
| Arbutus | Tung oil | Features laser-engraved branding and live edge details |
